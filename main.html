Bahubali: The Beginning is an epic historical action film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. The story follows Shivudu, a young man who discovers his royal heritage and learns about his father Amarendra Bahubali, a brave king who was betrayed and killed. Shivudu sets out on a journey to reclaim his kingdom and fulfill his destiny.
